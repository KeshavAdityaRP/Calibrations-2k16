CALIBRATIONS

OCTOBER 08th, 2016

ABOUT

What does the word “Calibration” mean? To put it in a simple way, it is the process of adjusting the base setting of a device to make it adhere to a specific standard in order for it to be used accurately. This is precisely the purpose of SVCE's Calibrations, an annual fun event conducted by Seniors for the Freshers. This is the day where you realize how fun college can be. This is the day that will enrich the bond between the seniors and you. In short, this is the day you become a true SVCE-ian. Participate in a variety of events, both solo and/or as a group. From dancing to cooking, gaming to music, and Dubsmash to photography, there are a multitude of events to let you show off your awesome skills and boost your street-cred [ ;) ].

All of the events will be conducted and judged by your very, very cool (trust me :p) seniors. Scroll down for the list of events and click on registration link provided in the navigation bar to know more about each event and to register your name(s) for the event. Drop by at SVCE on the 8th of October to #GetCalibrated! There are various cash prizes to be won as well.
